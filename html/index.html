Bottle Up

With a mission to reduce single use bottles and cups, we are introducing the BottleUp thermo bottle. With this bottle, you can fill up your drink of choice at selected retailers for a reduced price. This includes coffee, tea, soda and sparkling water.

Join us, and help us keep single use plastic out of landfills and waterways.

Learn more

Our highly functional bottle is designed with a triple-walled thermo technology, which keeps the temperature of your selected drink for several hours without affecting the temperature on the outside of the bottle. This technology also prevents condensation. With this bottle you can keep hot drinks warm for 10 hours, and cold drinks cold for 24 hours. Additionally, the mouth of the bottle is big enough to fit most ice cubes.

You can get the bottle in two different sizes, depending on your needs. The large bottle fits 750ml, the small one fits 500ml. Both sizes are available in a vide range of colours.
